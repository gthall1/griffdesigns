The Watercrystal

The Watercrystal is a high end glassware boutique based in London. I had the opportunity to redesign their corporate branding, design their logo, and lead design and code their new website. The target customer profiles were fascinating (think the yacht and private jet crowds), and I learned a ton about marketing, branding, and advertising as well. Certainly not my bread-and-butter, but I learned a lot and was challenged to develop new skills in a high pressure environment.
