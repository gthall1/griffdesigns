Shopboy

Shopboy is a high end fashion tailoring boutique in NYC and I worked with the founders to design their website. I'm so proud to have been a part of the design process and bring their vision to life while getting to play a small role in their huge success having been named the No. 1 tailoring shop in NYC in 2018. Some of the biggest challenges were information hierarchy, designing an easy appointment system for mobile, and improving user content flows when customers needed to communicate with management.
