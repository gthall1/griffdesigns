Salamone Associates

Salamone Associates is a very well established political consulting firm in the DC area. I had the opportunity to lead the design team creating their new corporate identity and web properties. It was an interesting challenge because the look and feel had to be very modern but the majority of their content from their 30+ years in business was really dated, so this created a challenging juxtaposition of new and old. Their client list is mind-blowing...visit their site and see for yourself!
