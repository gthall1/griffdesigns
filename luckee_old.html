Luckee

Luckee is a web app platform where users play free games with ads built into the landscape of the games and earn a portion of the revenue generated from the ads. I was a cofounder of Luckee and lead product design. I'm most proud of the API and AI features we built to programmatically swap game assets with company logos for advertising. Make sure and visit the site to see the cool windmill and shooting stars on the landing page!
